Product Rules & Regulations

Official Guidelines for Barangay Vendors

01

Product Quality Standards

All products must be safe, properly labeled, and in good condition before being sold to customers.

02

Pricing Transparency

All items must have clearly displayed and accurate prices. Hidden charges are strictly prohibited.

03

Prohibited Items

Selling illegal, expired, counterfeit, or unsafe products is strictly forbidden under barangay regulations.

04

Proper Packaging

All products must be properly sealed or packaged to ensure safety and prevent contamination or damage.

05

Customer Rights & Returns

Vendors must respect customer rights. Defective or misrepresented products should be eligible for return or replacement.
