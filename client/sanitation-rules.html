Sanitation & Cleanliness Rules

Official Guidelines for Barangay Vendors

01

Waste Management

All vendors must provide their own trash bins with liners. Segregation of biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste is mandatory.

02

Personal Hygiene

Vendors handling food must wear hairnets, face masks, and aprons at all times during operation.

03

Stall Maintenance

The immediate vicinity (3-meter radius) of the stall must be kept clean. Spills must be cleaned immediately to prevent accidents.

04

Prohibited Materials

The use of single-use plastics and styrofoam for serving food is strictly prohibited per City Ordinance.

06

Water Use & Hygiene

Only clean, potable water must be used for food preparation and utensil washing. Vendors must have a handwashing station with soap available at all times.

07

Pest Control

Stalls must be kept free from pests such as flies, rats, and cockroaches. Vendors must not leave food exposed when unattended.

08

Wastewater Disposal

Used water from washing dishes or food preparation must be disposed of in designated drainage areas only. Dumping wastewater on streets is prohibited.

09

Cleaning Schedule

Stalls must be cleaned before opening, during operation as needed, and thoroughly cleaned after closing each day.

10

Inspection Cooperation

Vendors must allow barangay or health inspectors to conduct random sanitation checks and comply with any corrective instructions.
